Research and publications

*Licentiate Thesis: My thesis deals with an experience of associativity in small cotton producers in the Pisco Valley in 2015.
*Master degree: My thesis examines the factors that enable or restrict the participation in sports or physical activities in adults in the United Kingdom in 2001 and 2015. This research helps us to understand sports participation in adults and identify certain gaps and inequalities that arise from its practice.
*Working paper: This working paper contains exercises and type cases carried out in the undergraduate course Quantitative Research Methods, which is taught at the Faculty of Administration and Executive Management of the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru (PUCP). *Publication: This publication examines the main changes and continuities that occurred in Perú between 1994 and 2012, taking into account the information from the National Agricultural Censuses of Peru
